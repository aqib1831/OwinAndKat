Multi-payment/Split Payment:

A new feature has been added in Netsolace Qs to accommodate multi-payments/split payments methods.

This new feature will allow customers to pay for transactions in the following combination payment methods:

  • Cash & Credit Cards
  • Cash & Gift Cards
  • Gift Cards and Credit Cards

This functionality has been provided in the form of a “Multi Payment” button on the Payment screen.

Processing: If a transaction is processed that requires the utilization of this method, and cash and credit card options are chosen, then the system automatically tenders the remaining amount from the 2nd option.

Refunds: When refunding an order that was paid by multiple payment mode, the system prompts to choose the refunding mode from original payment modes, where  only one payment mode can be selected to provide refund to customer.

 
Multi payment for Gift card transactions:

If an order is being paid via Gift card (GC) and the gift card balance is not sufficient to pay the whole order via GC, Qs system will automatically charge the entire amount available on Gift card and prompt for cash or credit card for the balance.

 
Batch reconciliation:

At dayend the online batch is reconciled, if it is not closed properly an alert is shown in Manager’s Menu showing the number of batches not closed properly with date and time.

 
Sales goals:

In Manager’s Menu a sales goal feature is added. In this section, Manager can set sales goal for a week. Manager will set the sales goal for selected week where QS will retrieves last year's same week data (if available) to show in the "COMP Data" and will automatically add 20% to those sales to provide new goals.

If there is no data from last year, then QS will check for any sales goal set in the past week.

Sales goals screen will be appear on QS homepage with Today’s sales goal where all employees can view their sales goals for the day with current status.
 
Store’s Average Ticket on Sales Goal Screen:

Store’s average ticket of the week will also be shown at the bottom of the Sales Goal screen.

 
Sales by Clerk Data on Sales Goal Screen:

Clicking on the ‘Details’ button on the “Today’s Goal” screen will display average ticket sales for all sales clerks separately .

 
Exempt tax:

On tax exempt transactions, two new fields, customer name and Tax ID , are provided to record customer information.

 
Check:

In payment options, Check payment mode has been changed to UDS .

 
Manage shift:

In Managers menu à Employee center, a new shift management capability has been provided.

A manager can add, edit, delete, activate or deactivate a shift. When adding a new employee, if a shift is added into the system it will be selected by default for that employee other wise if there is no shift the employee will not be assigned to any shift.  It is not a required field.

 
Reports:

Four new reports have been added in this update:      

 
Tax exempted orders:

This report shows the tax exempted orders with sales clerk name and the tax id. Reports data can be viewed by providing date range as search parameter.

 
Daily Sales recap report:

Real-time daily sales along with labor costs are shown within this report.

 
Store performance report:
 

Store performance report shows the store sales summary before the dayend has been performed for the store.

Sales summary can be viewed for one day or the whole week with weeks calculated totals.

 
Sales goal report:

Sales goals that are set from within the managers menu are shown with the expected and actual sales.
